Integrated Development Monitoring for Every Child

The Bright Futures Development Activity Tracker offers a structured way to capture milestones, behaviours and health events from infancy through adolescence, with a special focus on autism and physical illness.

It is an educational and monitoring tool to support early identification of developmental delays and guide timely intervention, not a replacement for clinical diagnosis or treatment.
